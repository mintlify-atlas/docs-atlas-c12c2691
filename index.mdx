  <Steps>
    <Step title="Create your account">
      Download the PARKINMX app from the App Store or Google Play. Sign up with your email or use biometric authentication for quick access.
      
      ```bash
      # iOS
      https://apps.apple.com/parkinmx
      
      # Android
      https://play.google.com/store/apps/parkinmx
      ```
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Add your vehicle">
      Register your vehicle by entering the license plate number. You can add multiple vehicles and switch between them when making reservations.
      
      <Note>
        Vehicle information is stored securely and used to verify your parking reservations.
      </Note>
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Fund your wallet">
      Add funds to your digital wallet using a credit or debit card. The minimum balance required for reservations is 120 credits.
      
      <Tip>
        Set up auto-reload to ensure you never miss a reservation due to low balance.
      </Tip>
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Make your first reservation">
      Select a parking spot on the interactive map, choose your arrival time, and confirm your reservation. You'll receive a QR code ticket instantly.
      
      ```typescript
      // Reservation flow
      1. Select spot from map (1-10)
      2. Choose date and time
      3. Select vehicle
      4. Confirm payment
      5. Receive QR ticket
      ```
      
      <Accordion title="Example reservation response">
        Your reservation is confirmed! You'll receive push notifications 15 minutes before your scheduled arrival time. The QR code ticket can be accessed from the home screen.
      </Accordion>
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</div>
