## Terms of Service

**Last Updated:** December 2025

By using PARKINMX, you agree to the following terms and conditions.

### Account Requirements

**To use PARKINMX, you must:**
- Be at least 18 years old
- Provide accurate registration information (name, email, phone)
- Have a valid payment method
- Own or have authorization to use the vehicles you register

<Warning>
Providing false information or using someone else's payment method is grounds for account termination.
</Warning>

### Reservation Policies

**Booking Rules:**
- Reservations must be made at least 1 hour in advance
- Operating hours: 7:00 AM - 11:00 PM daily
- Maximum 1 active reservation per user at a time
- Minimum wallet balance: 120 credits ($20 MXN)

**Cancellation Policy:**
- **Free cancellation:** More than 1 hour before reservation time
- **Late cancellation:** Less than 1 hour before = 100 credit penalty
- **No-show:** Failing to arrive = automatic cancellation + 100 credit penalty

**Late Arrival:**
- Your spot remains reserved until the end of your time slot
- No refund for unused time if you arrive late

### Payment and Credits

**Credit System:**
- 1 MXN = 6 credits
- Minimum recharge: 30 MXN (180 credits)
- Bonus credits: +20 credits for recharges ≥100 credits
- Credits are non-refundable and non-transferable

**Payment Processing:**
- All payments processed through Mercado Pago
- Card information encrypted and tokenized
- PARKINMX never stores full card numbers
- Charges appear as "PARKINMX" or "MercadoPago" on statements

<Info>
Unused credits remain in your wallet indefinitely and do not expire.
</Info>

### Prohibited Activities

You may **not**:
- Share your account credentials with others
- Transfer or sell your account
- Use the app for illegal parking or unauthorized vehicles
- Attempt to manipulate the credit system or reservation availability
- Reverse-engineer the app or QR code system
- Create fake accounts or friend requests for spam

**Violations may result in:**
- Immediate account suspension
- Forfeiture of wallet balance
- Legal action if damages occur

### Liability and Disclaimers

**PARKINMX is not responsible for:**
- Vehicle damage, theft, or loss while parked
- Personal injuries on parking premises
- Delays due to technical issues or server downtime
- Third-party payment processor failures
- GPS inaccuracy or mapping errors

<Warning>
PARKINMX provides a reservation platform only. Physical parking facility operators are responsible for premises security and vehicle safety.
</Warning>

**Service Availability:**
- We strive for 99.9% uptime but cannot guarantee uninterrupted service
- Scheduled maintenance will be announced 24 hours in advance
- Emergency maintenance may occur without notice

### Account Termination

**You may delete your account at any time:**
1. Go to **Perfil → Configuración**
2. Scroll to bottom and tap **"Eliminar Cuenta"**
3. Confirm deletion
4. All data is permanently deleted within 30 days

<Note>
Remaining credit balance is **forfeited** upon account deletion. Withdraw or use credits before deleting your account.
</Note>

**We may terminate accounts for:**
- Violation of terms of service
- Fraudulent payment activity
- Repeated no-shows or late cancellations
- Abusive behavior toward staff or other users

## Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

### Data We Collect

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Account Information">
    **Collected during registration:**
    - Full name (first and last name)
    - Email address
    - Username
    - Password (hashed, never stored in plain text)
    - Phone number (optional)
    - Date of birth (optional)
    - Gender (optional)
    - Profile photo (optional)
    
    **Purpose:** Account creation, authentication, communication
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Vehicle Information">
    **Stored for each vehicle:**
    - License plate number
    - Brand, model, color
    - Custom alias
    
    **Purpose:** Reservation verification, parking spot assignment
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Payment Information">
    **Tokenized data only:**
    - Last 4 digits of card number
    - Card type (Visa/Mastercard)
    - Expiration date
    - Cardholder name
    
    **Not stored:** Full card number, CVV
    
    **Purpose:** Processing payments via Mercado Pago
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Reservation History">
    **Logged for each reservation:**
    - Date and time
    - Parking spot number
    - Vehicle used
    - Duration
    - Payment card used
    - Credits charged
    - Status (completed/cancelled/no-show)
    
    **Purpose:** Transaction history, analytics, dispute resolution
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Location Data">
    **Collected when:**
    - Viewing parking map
    - Using "Cómo llegar" (directions)
    
    **Precision:** City-level only, not GPS coordinates
    
    **Purpose:** Display nearby parking locations, provide directions
    
    <Info>
    PARKINMX does **not** track your real-time location or movement patterns.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Device Information">
    **Automatically collected:**
    - Device model
    - Operating system version
    - App version
    - Device language
    - Push notification token
    
    **Purpose:** App compatibility, push notifications, crash reports
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Usage Analytics">
    **Tracked anonymously:**
    - Screen views
    - Button taps
    - Feature usage frequency
    - Session duration
    
    **Purpose:** Improve app experience, identify bugs
    
    **Tools:** Firebase Analytics (Google)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### How We Use Your Data

**Primary purposes:**
1. **Account Management** - Create and maintain your profile
2. **Reservations** - Process bookings and generate QR tickets
3. **Payments** - Handle credit purchases and wallet transactions
4. **Communication** - Send reservation confirmations, reminders, and support responses
5. **Security** - Detect fraud, prevent abuse, verify identity
6. **Analytics** - Understand usage patterns and improve features

**We will never:**
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Use your data for unrelated marketing purposes
- Share your location data with advertisers
- Access your contacts or photos without permission

### Data Sharing

We share limited data with:

**Third-Party Services:**
- **Firebase (Google)** - Database, authentication, analytics
- **Mercado Pago** - Payment processing
- **EmailJS** - Transactional email delivery
- **Expo** - Push notifications, app updates

**Legal Requirements:**
- Law enforcement (with valid warrant)
- Court orders or subpoenas
- Fraud investigations

<Warning>
We will notify you of data requests unless legally prohibited.
</Warning>

### Data Retention

**How long we keep your data:**

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Account information | Until account deletion |
| Reservation history | 7 years (tax/audit requirements) |
| Payment transactions | 10 years (financial regulations) |
| Support tickets | 3 years |
| Analytics data | 26 months (aggregated) |
| Deleted accounts | 30 days (recovery window) |

### Your Rights

**You have the right to:**
- **Access** - Request a copy of all data we hold about you
- **Correction** - Update inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Deletion** - Request account and data deletion ("Right to be Forgotten")
- **Portability** - Export your data in JSON format
- **Objection** - Opt out of marketing communications

**To exercise these rights:**
1. Go to **Perfil → Privacidad**
2. Select the appropriate option
3. Confirm your request
4. Data export delivered via email within 30 days

### Security Measures

**We protect your data with:**
- **Encryption in transit** - TLS 1.3 for all API communication
- **Encryption at rest** - Firebase data encrypted on disk
- **Authentication** - Multi-factor (PIN + email verification)
- **Access controls** - Role-based permissions for staff
- **Regular audits** - Quarterly security reviews
- **Secure backups** - Daily encrypted backups to separate location

<Tip>
**You can enhance your security by:**
- Enabling biometric authentication
- Using a strong password
- Changing your PIN regularly
- Logging out on shared devices
</Tip>

### Cookies and Tracking

**Mobile App:**
- No cookies used
- Anonymous analytics via Firebase
- Push notification tokens stored locally

**Web App:**
- Session cookies (essential for login)
- Analytics cookies (Google Analytics, opt-out available)
- No advertising or tracking cookies

### Children's Privacy

PARKINMX is **not intended for users under 18**. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover a user is under 18, we will:
1. Immediately suspend the account
2. Delete all associated data
3. Notify the email address on file

### International Users

**Data Storage Location:** Firebase servers (United States and Mexico)

**GDPR Compliance (EU users):**
- Right to access, correction, deletion
- Data processing agreement with Firebase
- EU-US Data Privacy Framework certified

**CCPA Compliance (California users):**
- Annual privacy notice
- Do Not Sell My Personal Information honored
- Right to deletion within 45 days

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as laws or practices change. You will be notified of material changes via:
- In-app notification
- Email to registered address
- Update banner on Terms screen

**Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.**
